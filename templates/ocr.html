OCR PDF Text Extraction

QuickPDFSum supports OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text from scanned or image-based PDFs. Whether your PDF is a scanned contract, handwritten note, or photographed document, the OCR engine can extract readable text.

Supported Languages

  • Korean (kor)
  • English (eng)
  • Mixed-language PDFs

Features

  • Accurate Korean OCR
  • No file storage — auto-deletion after extraction
  • Scanned PDF support
  • Fast processing
